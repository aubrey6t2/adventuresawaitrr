    • ABOUT US

    At Adventures Await Retreat, there’s plenty of space for camping! Sitting on 140 acres in Delaware, we are a clean campsite with lots to do. Enjoy mini golf, hiking trails, fishing ponds, our petting zoo, pool and spa, and miles of gorgeous forest-covered land. We are also close to local beaches, the casino, and no worries for those who don’t want to be too off the grid–we also have cell coverage in the area for all major providers. Who’s ready to spend some time outdoors–around the campfire and beneath the stars?
